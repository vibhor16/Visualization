#About the Dataset

Name - Avocado Dataset
Features 10 numerical features which are -

1) Population
2) Price
3) Total Volume
4) Total Bags
5) PLU4046
6) PLU4225
7) PLU4770
8) Small Bags
9) Large Bags
10) XLarge Bags

Rows: There are 1000 rows in the dataset

Following operations are performed - 

1. Data clustering and decimation

2. Dimension reduction on both org and 2 types of reduced data

3. Visualization of both original and 2 types of reduced data
