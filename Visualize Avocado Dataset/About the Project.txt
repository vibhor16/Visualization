Visualizing the Avocado Dataset

YouTube Link
The video can be found at the following link - https://youtu.be/i5x7kOHrEX8

The primary dataset used is the “Avocado Prices in US Markets” found on Kaggle - https://www.kaggle.com/neuromusic/avocado-prices.

The Avocado dataset had 14 features out of which 12 features were filtered out.
The dataset on Cities (found on https://simplemaps.com/data/world-cities) was fused with the Avocado dataset to provide the additional 3 columns which are the details of the cities the Avocado Market is in.
The dataset has 6 categorical features –
1) Type
2) Year
3) City
4) Rating
5) Major Size
6) Population

The dataset has 9 numerical features –
1) Price
2) Total Volume
3) Total Bags
4) PLU4046
5) PLU4225
6) PLU4770
7) Small Bags
8) Large Bags
9) XLarge Bags
